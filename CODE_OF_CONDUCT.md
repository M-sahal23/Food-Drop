# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We expect all community members to be respectful, inclusive, and professional.

## Expected Behavior

Be:
- **Respectful** of diverse opinions and backgrounds
- **Inclusive** and welcoming to all
- **Professional** in all communications
- **Constructive** in feedback and criticism
- **Honest** and transparent
- **Collaborative** and team-oriented

## Examples of Encouraged Behavior

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Helping others learn and grow

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable and will not be tolerated:

- **Harassment**: Offensive comments related to race, gender, sexual orientation, disability, mental illness, etc.
- **Bullying**: Intimidation, threats, or aggressive behavior
- **Disrespect**: Intentionally disruptive behavior or inflammatory language
- **Exclusion**: Deliberate exclusion based on identity or characteristics
- **Abuse**: Sexual, violent, or hateful language and imagery
- **Spam**: Unwanted promotional content or mass messaging
- **Privacy Violation**: Sharing personal information without consent

## Reporting & Enforcement

### Reporting a Violation
If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:

1. **Document** the incident (date, time, details)
2. **Report** to: conduct@example.com
3. **Include**: Description, witnesses, context
4. **Do not** publicly shame or escalate

### Investigation Process
1. We will review and investigate reports
2. Confidentiality will be maintained
3. We may contact you for clarification
4. Appropriate action will be taken

### Consequences
Violations may result in:
- Warning
- Comment removal
- Temporary ban
- Permanent ban

## Our Responsibilities

Community leaders are responsible for:
- Clarifying expectations
- Enforcing standards fairly
- Taking appropriate corrective action
- Protecting reporter confidentiality

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project spaces (GitHub, issues, discussions)
- Official events and meetings
- Public community spaces
- Private communications in project context
