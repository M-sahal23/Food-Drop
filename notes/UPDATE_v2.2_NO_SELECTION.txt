✨ USER BENEFITS:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. ACCIDENT RECOVERY:
   └─ Accidentally clicked "Large Size"?
   └─ Just click "❌ No Size" to deselect
   └─ No need to reload page

2. SIMPLER INTERFACE:
   └─ No confusing quantity spinners
   └─ Simple yes/no selection
   └─ Cleaner look and feel

3. FASTER ORDERING:
   └─ Fewer fields to interact with
   └─ Quicker decision making
   └─ Better mobile experience

4. NO CONFUSION:
   └─ Each option = 1 unit
   └─ No quantity math
   └─ Straightforward pricing
