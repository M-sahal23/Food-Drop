🎯 HOW IT WORKS:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SIZE OPTIONS (Radio Buttons):
  ○ ❌ No Size       ← Click to deselect if you clicked by accident
  ○ Regular
  ○ Medium
  ○ Large

BASE OPTIONS (Radio Buttons):
  ○ ❌ No Base       ← Click to deselect if you clicked by accident
  ○ Maida
  ○ Wheat

SAUCE OPTIONS (Radio Buttons):
  ○ ❌ No Sauce      ← Click to deselect if you clicked by accident
  ○ Garlic Sauce
  ○ Red Sauce
  ○ Pesto

TOPPINGS OPTIONS (Checkboxes):
  ☑ Cheese          ← Check/uncheck to select/deselect
  ☐ Pepperoni
  ☐ Onions
  ☐ Mushrooms


💡 USER BENEFITS:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. ACCIDENT RECOVERY:
   └─ Oops! Clicked "Large Size" by mistake?
   └─ Just click "❌ No Size" to deselect
   └─ Don't need to reload page or clear form

2. SIMPLER INTERFACE:
   └─ No confusing quantity spinners
   └─ Simple select/deselect flow
   └─ Cleaner, less cluttered page

3. FASTER ORDERING:
   └─ Fewer decisions to make
   └─ Fewer fields to interact with
   └─ Better on mobile devices

4. CLEAR PRICING:
   └─ Each selected option = 1 unit
   └─ No quantity math
   └─ Straightforward pricing
