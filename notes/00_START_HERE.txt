🔧 TECHNICAL DETAILS:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

FORM INPUT NAMES:
  • Sauce: sauce_qty_<sauce_id>
  • Toppings: topping_qty_<topping_id>

BACKEND LOGIC:
  • save_customization() reads quantities
  • Adds toppings multiple times (ManyToMany)
  • Calculates final price with all quantities

PRICE CALCULATION:
  total = base_price
  + (size_price if selected)
  + (sauce_price × sauce_qty if selected)
  + sum(topping_price × topping_qty for each topping)
  = item_total
  × main_quantity
  = final_total
