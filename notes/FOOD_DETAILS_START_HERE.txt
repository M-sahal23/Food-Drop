⚙️ TECHNICAL DETAILS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

URL PATTERN:
  • Route: /foods/<int:id>/
  • View: views.Food_Details
  • Template: fooddetails.html
  • Example: /foods/5/ shows food with id=5

FORM SUBMISSION:
  • Method: POST
  • Target: /foods/cart/<int:id>
  • Parameter: quantity (hidden input)
  • Result: Item added to cart, redirects to cart page

DATABASE USAGE:
  • Model: FoodItems (for food data)
  • Model: Cart (for cart storage)
  • Query: Single query per page load (optimized)

❓ FAQ
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: How do users access the food details page?
A: By clicking on any food item from the Home page or All Foods page.

Q: What happens when they click "Add to Cart"?
A: The product is added to their cart with the selected quantity, and they're
   redirected to the cart page.

Q: What happens when they click "Customize"?
A: They're taken to the customization page where they can select options like
   size, base, sauce, and toppings based on the food category.

Q: Is it mobile-responsive?
A: Yes! The page is fully responsive and works on mobile, tablet, and desktop.

Q: Can users change the quantity?
A: Yes! They can use the +/- buttons or type the number directly.

Q: What if the food has no description?
A: A fallback message "No description available" is shown.

Q: What if the food has no image?
A: A default placeholder image is shown.

Q: Does it require any database changes?
A: No! It uses the existing FoodItems and Cart models.
